Problem statement

Headline: Many college students lack simple tools to manage their spending and build healthy budgeting habits, leading to stress and poor financial decisions.

College Students often struggle to keep track of their spending and manage money wisely. Without simple tools or guidance, it's easy to overspend, fall behind on bills, or miss oppurtunies to save. A better way to track expenses and build smart budgeting habits could help students stay financially stable and stress-free.
